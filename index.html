Privacy Policy

App: All-in-One Calculator (com.allinone.calculator.plus)
Last updated: July 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how All-in-One Calculator ("the app", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

By using the app, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy. Where consent is required by applicable law, we will request consent separately.

We do not sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Data You Create in the App

Calculations you save, favorites you mark, and app settings and preferences are generally stored locally on your device.

Account Data

If you choose to create an account and enable cloud synchronization, we may process your email address and synced calculator data through our backend provider so that your data can be synchronized across your devices.

Diagnostic Data

We may collect crash reports and error diagnostics, including information such as device model, operating system version, app version, error information, and a randomly generated identifier used to group related crash events.

Advertising Data

When advertisements are shown in the free version of the app, our advertising provider may process advertising identifiers, ad interaction information, and related technical data to serve and measure advertisements.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide the app's calculator functionality.
  • Store your saved calculations, favorites, and preferences.
  • Synchronize your data across devices when you enable an account.
  • Diagnose crashes and technical problems.
  • Improve the reliability and security of the app.
  • Display and measure advertising in the free version.
  • Process in-app purchases and manage premium access.
  • Respond to privacy, support, and account deletion requests.

3. Legal Bases for EEA and UK Users

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we process personal data on the following legal bases, depending on the circumstances:

  • Performance of a contract: to provide app functionality and account-related services.
  • Consent: where consent is required, including certain personalized advertising activities.
  • Legitimate interests: to maintain app security, diagnose technical issues, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.
  • Legal obligations: where processing is required by applicable law.

Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw your consent at any time through the available privacy controls in the app.

5. Data Storage, Retention, and Security

Calculator history, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device by default.

If you enable an account and cloud synchronization, the corresponding data may be stored with our backend provider and retained until you delete it or delete your account, subject to applicable legal requirements.

Crash diagnostics may be retained by our diagnostic service provider according to its applicable retention policies.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect information, including encryption in transit where supported by our service providers. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Advertising and Consent

The free version of the app may display advertisements through Google AdMob.

Where required by law, we request consent before serving personalized advertisements.

If you decline consent or have not provided consent, the app requests non-personalized advertisements where supported by the applicable advertising configuration.

You may be able to change your advertising privacy choices through the privacy settings available in the app.

Purchasing the premium or ad-free version removes advertisements where the purchased product provides that benefit.

7. Account Deletion and Your Choices

You may have the following choices, depending on the features available in your version of the app:

  • Export your data using the in-app export feature, if available.
  • Delete local app data by using the available in-app data controls or by clearing the app's data through your device settings.
  • Delete your account and associated synced data through: Settings → Account → Delete Account, if this feature is available in the app.
  • Contact us by email to request account deletion.
  • Manage advertising consent through the privacy controls available in the app.

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data. You may also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can investigate and take appropriate action.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

If material changes are made, we may also provide additional notice through the app or other appropriate means.
