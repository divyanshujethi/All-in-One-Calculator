Delete Your Account

This page explains how users of All-in-One Calculator can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the All-in-One Calculator app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open Account.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the account deletion request.

Alternative Request by Email

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by emailing:

divyanshujethi@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your All-in-One Calculator account so that we can identify the account to be deleted.

What Data Is Deleted?

When your account is deleted, we delete the account information associated with your account and the cloud-synced calculator data associated with that account, subject to applicable legal requirements.

This may include:

  • Your account email address
  • Your cloud-synced calculator data
  • Account-related profile information

What May Be Retained?

Certain information may be retained where necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud or abuse, or maintain transaction and accounting records as required by law.

Purchase and transaction records may be retained by payment and purchase service providers, such as Google Play or RevenueCat, according to their own policies and applicable legal requirements.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days. In-app deletion requests may be processed sooner where technically possible.

Important Information

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. You may lose access to cloud-synced data associated with the account.
